02 · Imagery

One prop,
one slide.

The 3D library does the heavy lifting. A single prop per slide — cropped past the edge — gives every page a strong visual anchor without crowding the copy.

  • Cobalt-blue plastic or clear glass
  • 3/4 view, never head-on
  • Soft contact shadow underneath
  • Always bleeding past one edge
